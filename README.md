# Customer Experience & Order Management Transformation (CRM Implementation Project)

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end Business Analysis and Project Delivery approach for a CRM-driven transformation of a customer order management process within a service-based retail environment.

The focus is on improving customer experience, centralising order management, and introducing structured CRM processes to replace manual and fragmented communication channels.

## Business Context

The current process relies on informal communication channels such as social media, email, and messaging applications to manage customer enquiries and orders.

This results in:

* Lack of visibility across customer interactions
* Manual and inconsistent quotation processes
* Limited order tracking capabilities
* Inefficient communication between stakeholders
* No centralised reporting or analytics

## Project Objective

To design and deliver a CRM-based solution that enables:

* Centralised customer and order management
* Standardised enquiry and quotation processes
* Improved customer experience and response times
* End-to-end order lifecycle visibility
* Data-driven reporting and insights

## Scope

### In Scope

* Customer enquiry management
* Order lifecycle tracking
* Quotation process standardisation
* Customer communication tracking
* CRM configuration and process design
* Reporting and dashboards

### Out of Scope

* Financial accounting systems
* Inventory automation
* Supply chain system integration

## Stakeholders

* Business Owner
* Customers
* Operations / Production Team
* Delivery Partners
* CRM Administrator / Technical Team

## Delivery Approach

A hybrid Agile and Waterfall approach will be used:

* Waterfall for initial requirements and design
* Agile iterations for CRM configuration and testing

## Success Criteria

* Centralised capture of 100% customer enquiries
* Reduced manual tracking of orders
* Improved response time to customer requests
* Increased operational visibility
* Structured and scalable CRM process design
