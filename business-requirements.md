🔷 1. Page Overview

Write:

This document defines the business requirements for a CRM-driven Customer Experience & Order Management Transformation initiative aimed at improving customer enquiry handling, order tracking, and operational efficiency.

🔷 2. Business Problem
Customer enquiries handled via fragmented channels (WhatsApp, Instagram, email)
No centralised customer data
Manual quotation and order tracking
Lack of visibility across order lifecycle
Limited reporting capability
🔷 3. Objectives
Centralise customer and order data in CRM
Standardise enquiry-to-order process
Improve customer response time
Enable end-to-end order tracking
Improve reporting and decision-making
🔷 4. Scope
In Scope
Customer enquiry management
Quotation process
Order lifecycle tracking
CRM configuration (Salesforce)
Reporting dashboards
Out of Scope
Accounting system integration
Inventory automation
Manufacturing systems
🔷 5. Stakeholders
Business Owner
Customers
Operations / Production Team
CRM / Technical Team
Delivery Partners
